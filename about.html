Origin Story

At H-Thai ML, you will savor flavorful dishes rarely served outside of Thailand! H-Thai ML specializes in sophisticated, little-known cuisine originally prepared for Thai royalty in generations past. We want to introduce a new generation to these classically-prepared dishes and ensure they won't be lost. In fact, our mission at H-Thai ML is to conserve Thai royal cuisine and share our love for this wonderful food with you. Our first H-Thai Ml resturant was opened in 1994 by first generation Thai immigrants chef, from Dusit, Bankok, Thailand, living in Fresh Meadows, Queens, NY.
